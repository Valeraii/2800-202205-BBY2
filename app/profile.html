Game Rules
  • Draw Letters (number of letters you get depends on difficulty
  • Combine letters to form a word and place it on the board
  • Use the bonus tile when forming a word to get bonus points
  • A new board and selection of letters will be available each day
  • Every player gets same word + same board everyday, Compete with your friends to see who can get the highest score
